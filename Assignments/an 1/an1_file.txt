Newspaper Agency Management System

Consider an information system for a newspaper agency management system. The agency distributes newspapers across several neighborhoods. It has several salesmen to do the job. A manager assigns one or more neighborhood to each salesman.

A customer can subscribe more than one newspapers. Secondly it may ask for different newspapers on different days. An invoice is generated at the end of each month. If a customer does not pay the bill, his subscription is cancelled.
